Linguix vs. Grammarly:
Which One is Best for Grammar Checker?

By Sheikh Shakil Dec 07, 2024

There are a lot of grammar checker tools available on the market. So, which one should you use? In this article, we'll compare Linguix and Grammarly to help you decide which tool is best for you.

Linguix is a relatively new grammar checker tool that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to identify errors in your writing. On the other hand, Grammarly is a more established tool that uses rules-based checking.

Both Linguix and Grammarly are effective at catching grammar mistakes. However, they differ in terms of features, pricing, and accuracy. Here's a closer look at each tool to help you decide which one is right for you.

Linguix List of Features:

Linguix offers many features that make it an excellent grammar checker tool, including:

Chrome browser extension: The Linguix extension for Google Chrome can be used to check your grammar as you type.

Performance Statistics Report: Linguix generates a report that shows your most common mistakes and your progress over time.

In-depth explanations: When you make a mistake, Linguix provides an in-depth explanation of the grammar rule to avoid making the same mistake in the future.

Team collaboration: Linguix allows you to invite team members to collaborate on a document. This is an excellent feature if you're working on a project with others and need to ensure that everyone's writing is up to par.

Auto-correction: Linguix can automatically correct errors in your writing.

Punctuation checker: The Linguix punctuation checker can help you ensure that you're using proper punctuation.

Linguix Pricing Model :

Linguix offers three pricing plans:

  • Free: The free plan includes all of the features listed above. However, it is limited to three checks per day.

  • Premium: The premium plan costs $14.99 per month and includes unlimited grammar checks.

  • Team: The Team plan costs $29.99 per month and includes all premium plan features, plus additional features for couples, such as collaboration tools and integrations.

    • Grammarly List of Features:

    Grammarly also offers many features that make it an excellent grammar checker tool, including:

  • Online Editor: The Grammarly online editor can be used to check your grammar as you write.

  • Multi-Browser extension: The Grammarly extension is available for multiple browsers, including Google Chrome, Safari, and Firefox.

  • Microsoft Office add-in: The Grammarly add-in for Microsoft Office can be used to check your grammar in Word, Outlook, and PowerPoint.

  • Grammarly Keyboard: The Grammarly Keyboard app can be used to check your grammar as you type on your mobile device.

  • Plagiarism detector: The Grammarly plagiarism detector can help you ensure that your writing is original.

  • Vocabulary suggestion: The Grammarly vocabulary suggestion tool can help you expand your vocabulary and improve your writing.

  • Multiple device document access: Grammarly allows you to access your documents from various devices.

    • Grammarly Pricing Model:

    Grammarly offers three pricing plans.

  • Free: The free plan includes all of the features listed above. However, it is limited to three checks per day.

  • Premium: The premium plan costs $12.00 per month and includes additional features, such as a plagiarism detector and a vocabulary suggestion tool.

  • Business: The Business plan costs $15.00 per month and includes all premium plan features, plus additional features for companies, such as team collaboration and document sharing.

    Linguix vs. Grammarly: Feature Comparison

    Bottom Line:

    Both Linguix and Grammarly are great grammar checker tools. However, they differ in terms of features, pricing, and accuracy. If you're looking for a tool with more features, Grammarly is a good option. If you're looking for a tool that is more accurate, Linguix is a good option. Written and Designed by: Sheikh Shakil
